Potasfield Foundation School is a great citadel of learning that has distinguished itself in the league of Nursery and Primary Schools in Lagos state at large. This is a School Founded by Elder A. U. Kalu through the inspiration of the Holy Spirit. The school was officially opened on the 1st February, 2008. The school commenced its first academic session on Monday, 4th February, 2008.

Working together with God in developing the Hidden Divine Potentials both Physically and Spiritually of the total individual in line with God’s principles through the impartation of knowledge.

To instill in students the Virtues that last including sound academic, entrepreneurial and moral skills that will place them on a firm pedestal for the challenges of their generation.

The college has been Fully Certified and Registered by The West African Examinations Council (WAEC) and National Examinations Council (NECO) in 2013 and 2014 respectively. The college stands out as a result of her commitment to her founding principle: “For Virtues That Last”. Our determination in focusing on our core values and objectives would in the long run impact our Local Community and Nation at large as well as the world, placing the College on a firm footing as one of the leading schools in the environment. Potasfield College is a popular choice and the students enjoy their experiences which is second to none.
